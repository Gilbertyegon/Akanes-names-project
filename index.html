Akan Names Projects

This is a web application that autogenerates a user's Akan name based on the Akan culture. The Akan names are usually based on the specific day on which an individual was born.Through this web application, you can fill in your birth date,month and year through a form and submit it. Once you do so, you will be able to see your Akan name based on your gender
